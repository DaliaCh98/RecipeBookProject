Name: Microwave Rice Pudding
Category: Desserts
Preparation Time: 15
--------------------------
Ingredients:
1/4 cup pudding rice
2 cups milk
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
extra cinnamon for dusting
--------------------------
Instructions:
In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the rice, milk, sugar, and cinnamon. Microwave on high for 5 minutes. Stir and continue to microwave for another 5-7 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes, until the rice pudding has thickened. Let it cool slightly and dust with extra cinnamon before serving.
