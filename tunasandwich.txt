Name: Tuna Salad Sandwich
Category: Meals
Preparation Time: 10
-------------------------
Ingredients:
1 can tuna in water, drained
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon mustard (optional)
a little chopped onion or celery (optional)
salt
pepper
2 slices of bread
------------------------
Instructions:
In a bowl, mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, mustard (if using), onion or celery (if using), salt, and pepper. Spread the tuna mixture onto the bread slices. You can eat the sandwiches as is or lightly toast them.
