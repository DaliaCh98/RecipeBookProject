Name: Yogurt Berry Parfait
Category: Desserts
Preparation Time: 10
(plus chilling time)
-------------------------
Ingredients:
1 cup Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
--------------------------
Instructions:
In a bowl, stir together the yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract. Divide the mixture into two glasses or bowls. Top with the frozen berries. You can chill for 15-20 minutes before serving.
