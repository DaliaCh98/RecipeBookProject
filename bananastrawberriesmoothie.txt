Name: Banana-Strawberry Smoothie
Category: Breakfast
Preparation Time: 5
------------------------
Ingredients:
1 ripe banana
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional).
------------------------
Instructions:
Put all the ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth and homogenous. Add more milk if needed for desired consistency. Serve immediately.
