Name: Pasta with Tomato Sauce and Basil
Category: Meals
Preparation Time: 25
--------------------------
Ingredients:
200g pasta
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
400g canned chopped tomatoes
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
salt
pepper
grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
--------------------------
Instructions:
Cook the pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and saut√© for 1 minute. Pour in the tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes. Stir in the basil just before the end. Drain the pasta and toss with the sauce. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
