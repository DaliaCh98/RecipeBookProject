Name: Chicken and Vegetable Salad
Category: Meals
Preparation Time: 20
(if chicken is pre-cooked)
-----------------------------
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked chicken
shredded or diced
2 cups mixed green vegetables
1/2 cucumber, diced
1/2 tomato, chopped
1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt
pepper
----------------------------
Instructions:
Place the greens, cucumber, and tomato in a bowl. Add the chicken and red onion. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
