Name: Scrambled Eggs with Whole Wheat Toast
Category: Breakfast
Preparation Time: 10
--------------------
Ingredients:
2 eggs,
1 tablespoon milk (optional)
1 teaspoon butter or olive oil
2 slices whole wheat bread
salt 
pepper
-------------------
Instructions:
Whisk the eggs in a bowl with milk (if using), salt, and pepper. Heat the butter or olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour in the eggs and gently stir with a spatula until cooked but still slightly moist. Toast the bread slices and serve the scrambled eggs on or next to them.
