Name: Vegetable Fried Rice
Category: Meals
Preparation Time: 20
--------------------------
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked rice (preferably day-old)
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1/2 cup frozen mixed vegetables
1 egg, lightly beaten
a little chopped fresh scallion (for garnish)
---------------------------
Instructions:
Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Pour in the egg and cook, scrambling it into small pieces. Remove the egg from the skillet and set aside. Add the vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 5 minutes. Add the rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes. Add the cooked egg back in and mix. Garnish with fresh scallion.
