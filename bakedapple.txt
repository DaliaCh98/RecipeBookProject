Name: Baked Apple with Cinnamon
Category: Desserts
Preparation Time: 30
-------------------------
Ingredients:
1 apple
1/2 teaspoon butter
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
-------------------------
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Cut the apple in half and remove the core. Place the apples in a small baking dish. Put a little butter in the cavity of each apple and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar (if using). Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the apples are tender. Serve warm or slightly cooled.
